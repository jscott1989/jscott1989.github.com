← Don't work for Powerhouse Direct

Get an email from "Powerhouse Direct Southampton" letting you know how interesting they find your CV and inviting you for an immediate interview? Sorry but your CV isn't THAT impressive...

The way it works is Powerhouse Direct will invite lots and lots of people to interview (just look at the amount of job adverts) and miraculously most of these get hired! that's because the job is 100% commission and you'll be required to sell rubbish products to people door-to-door (for below minimum wage as you're a "contractor"). As such they have a very high turnover of staff.

You can read more about this company here

Other signs that it's shady: they email from a Gmail address, their website isn't set up, etc. etc. I'd recommend just ignoring them if they invite you to interview.

Consider this a public service :) Hopefully this can chart on Google results for Powerhouse Direct.
