I've been working at Wazoku since the start of 2012. The main product is Idea Spotlight, an idea management system which creates communities around ideas and encourages innovation within an organisation

The work varies day to day. Normally there'll be work on Idea Spotlight, but also work on Competitions, or even writing an AI competition.

The bulk of the work is in Python using the Django framework, though we also use quite a bit of Coffeescript and some Clojure, which keeps things interesting.
