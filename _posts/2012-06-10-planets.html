Planets is the last game I'm working on as an entry to SA Gamedev VII. The theme is "balls" and though I'm not sure how well my idea matches with the theme, this is the best I could come up with so here we go!

The game focuses heavily on physics, but current experiments show that the making the physics too realistic makes the game impossible to play. I'll need to figure out if I can simplify the physics while keeping the game challenging, or come up with a new idea.

I'm working with Alan Longstaff on this game.
