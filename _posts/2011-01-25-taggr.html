Taggr is a project I started while studying at the University of Southampton which aims to bring Facebook-style photo tagging to the open web.

It runs on the Google App Engine with the server coded in Python and the client coded in Javascript using Google Closure Tools.

It has been put on hold for the time being due to other commitments but I do hope to get back to it.
